What's happening in Maven's world

Maven Dominates Competitors with September Release

California Rulesets: Maven is excited to add two new Hours of Service rulesets to support drivers who drive in California. The two new rulesets, California Passenger and California Property, enable qualifying drivers to take advantage of California’s special hours of service rules.

NEW Proximity Search Feature: The Proximity Search feature allows fleet managers to search for vehicles within a radius of 0.5mi to 5mi of a specific address or city and for a specific date. Managers can select any of the returned results to see the movement of that vehicle within the selected radius.
